In the journey of life and career, certain phrases have resonated with me, shaping my perspective and approach. Here, I share some of the mottos that have become my personal guiding principles, along with a glimpse into how they've influenced my path.



---



## Elon Musk

> *"Have a problem to solve instead of learning the tools to solve."*

Elon Musk's advice emphasises the importance of being driven by purpose. In my own journey as a software engineer, this mindset has shifted my focus from merely acquiring technical skills to applying them creatively to solve real-world challenges.


> *"There need to be reasons to get up in the morning."*

Finding purpose and motivation in life is crucial. This quote reminds me of the importance of setting meaningful goals, whether it's innovating in the tech industry or contributing positively to society.


---

## Andrew Tate

> *"Everything is rented, you can't start slipping."*

This quote serves as a powerful reminder that success and achievements are transient. It motivates me to continuously strive for excellence and not to become complacent in my endeavors.


> *"If you actually try your best, you can't lose."*

This motto instills the belief that true success lies in effort and dedication. It has taught me to measure my achievements by the sincerity of my efforts rather than the outcome.


---


## Jonathan Ma

> *"Programming is a tool to turn ideas into reality. Use it as an opportunity to unleash creativity and build something remarkable."*

As a software engineer, this quote resonates deeply with me. It highlights the power of programming as a medium for creativity and innovation, inspiring me to build impactful and meaningful projects.


---


## Burken Scott in the book "Make Things Happen"

> *"The brave are those who feel fear but choose to take action anyway."*

Courage is not the absence of fear, but the ability to move forward in spite of it. This motto has encouraged me to face challenges head-on, whether in my professional or personal life.
