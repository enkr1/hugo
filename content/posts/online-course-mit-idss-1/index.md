## What's the Deal with DS & AI?
So, what exactly are Data Science and AI? They are like the superheroes of the tech world. ## Quizzes
I have compiled some practical casual quizzes along the way during the session, I thought it would be fun to share as well:

1. **Inferential Data Science**:
   - **Question**: What's the scoop on the inferential aspect of data science?
   - **Answer**: It's about using stats to guess stuff about a whole population from a tiny sample. Super handy for predicting trends and behaviours.

2. **Limits of Guesswork**:
   - **Question**: What trips up inferential thinking?
   - **Answer**: You need good data and solid know-how of the subject. Missing these? Well, your guesses might be way off.

3. **Computational Paradigm**:
   - **Question**: What's a big deal about the computational approach in data science?
   - **Answer**: It's all about letting computers do the heavy lifting to process and analyse data faster and more accurately.

4. **Aiming High in Computations**:
   - **Question**: What's the endgame for computational data science?
   - **Answer**: To cook up some smart algorithms that streamline how we handle and make sense of data.

5. **When Things Get Tricky**:
   - **Question**: What's the headache with computational thinking?
   - **Answer**: The bigger or more complex the data, the tougher the computing challenge. You need some serious computing power to tackle it.

6. **Example Time!**:
   - **Question**: Can you think of a real-world computational headache?
   - **Answer**: Think about planning the quickest delivery routes for trucks or finding the fastest flight path. Yep, that's the kind of puzzle we solve here.
