On a serene evening on 19 April 2024, I found myself amongst a keen audience in a local auditorium, all of us buzzing with anticipation for a talk by Lim Hock Chee, the visionary CEO of Sheng Siong. Thanks to a kind invite from my friend, personal wealth manager, [Troy Lai](https://sg.linkedin.com/in/troy-lai-990108198), this wasn't just another evening. It was a doorway to invaluable insights from one of Singapore's top business leaders. Speaking eloquently in Mandarin, Mr. Lim shared his wisdom on personal ethics and business acumen with a candour that was both enlightening and refreshing.

![Lim Hock Chee speaking at the event]("photo_2024-04-20_17-52-05.jpg")

<!-- more -->

## Key Takeaways from Lim Hock Chee's Talk

### The Essence of Integrity and Kindness (善良和正直的重要性)
Mr. Lim stressed, **"Being kind and doing the right thing are fundamental to gaining respect and success."** This resonated deeply, reaffirming my belief that integrity is crucial in all relationships, both personal and professional.

**Personal Reflection:**
This talk has strengthened my resolve to live by these values more diligently, ensuring they reflect in every decision and interaction.


### The Power of Self-Reflection (先了解自己，再了解他人)
One of the standout themes was the importance of self-awareness. Understanding ourselves paves the way for genuine self-improvement and empathetic leadership.

**Personal Plan:**
Inspired, I aim to set aside time whenever I have time at night or each week for self-reflection, to better understand my motivations and fine-tune my responses to challenges.


### Cultivating a Positive Work Culture (培养积极的工作文化)
Passionately, Mr. Lim discussed the critical role of fostering a positive work environment where leaders embody the principles they advocate. This is essential for inspiring innovation and collective advancement.

**Workplace Application:**
I'm inspired to further this positive culture at my workplace, encouraging creativity and supporting my team to think innovatively.


### Entrepreneurship and Team Building (创业与团队建设)
The story of starting small and dreaming big, as shared by Lim Hock Chee, was particularly compelling. He highlighted the importance of building a team that aligns with your vision.

**Inspired Action:**
Moved by his narrative, I will soon to plan and kick-start a side project I've been dreaming about. It's more than a business venture; it's about creating a community united by a shared vision and passion and I always want to build something remarkable and that brings value to the world.


## Conclusion
Lim Hock Chee's insights extend beyond mere business tactics; they are profound life lessons. His vitality and attention to detail in storytelling offered a masterclass in passion and perseverance. His journey exemplifies the dedication needed to achieve excellence—a beacon for my path forward.

These lessons transcend boundaries, offering universal insights. As I integrate these into my daily life, I'm inspired by the transformative power of integrity and proactive leadership.
